Welcome to manybody.org

 

Exploring the dynamics of dense stellar systems

Many projects, many participants:
  • Starlab -- a software environment for stellar dynamics and stellar evolution
  • Special-purpose computers for stellar dynamics -- the GRAPE project
  • NEMO -- A Stellar Dynamics Toolbox
  • Visualization in the Digital Dome of the Hayden Planetarium
  • Virtual Director visualization software at NCSA
  • Linking simulations and visualization (partiview, TVO)
  • TVO toy models (as part of one of the NVO initiatives)
You can already check out the newly designed manybody.org, which we experiment with on another machine.
