NEMO SUMMARY

HISTORY

NEMO was written around the first treecode in 1986/87 by Joshua Barnes, Piet Hut and Peter Teuben, while at the Institute for Advanced Study. Since then, it has expanded and included many utilities to initialize, integrate, analyze and visualize N-body data, and various related types of data, such as Orbits, Images, FITS files, and Tables.
