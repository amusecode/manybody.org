Organizers:

    Enrico Vesperini (Drexel University)
    Steve McMillan (Drexel University)
    Piet Hut (Institute for Advanced Study)

Agenda

This small informal workshop will discuss the current and proposed development of the MUSE software framework, as well as some of the scientific problems MUSE is designed to tackle. The workshop will start with an introductory talk on the status of MUSE development by Steve McMillan, and will continue in a roundtable/forum format with plenty of time for free discussion on technical and scientific aspects of MUSE.

There will be no registration fee, but please send an e-mail to vesperin@physics.drexel.edu if you are planning on attending.
